About Realingo

Our Mission

Realingo is an innovative language learning platform that combines the power of AI-generated storytelling with interactive language exercises. Our mission is to create an engaging, personalized language learning experience that adapts to each user's needs and interests.

How It Works

Realingo creates personalized mini-stories where you play the protagonist establishing a life abroad. Each story is tailored to your chosen language field (e.g., general, medical) and adapts its complexity based on your proficiency level.

As you progress through the stories, you'll encounter interactive exercises that reinforce your learning. After completing each mini-story, you'll unlock dedicated vocabulary training sessions to review what you've learned.

Each completed mini-story contributes to your ongoing language learning "novel," creating a continuous narrative that reflects your growth and progress.

Key Features

  • Story-Driven Learning: Engage with mini-stories that unlock sequentially as you progress
  • Field-Specific Content: Learn vocabulary relevant to your chosen field
  • Adaptive Dialogue: Language complexity adjusts to your proficiency level
  • Post-Story Training: Dedicated vocabulary practice sessions after each story
  • In-Story Mentoring: Get guidance and vocabulary tips as you learn
  • Competitive Role Play: Practice with others in 1 vs. 1 battles
